The Thing(1982)

The ultimate of alien terror, John Carpanter's 1982 critically panned to critically revered movie in the years since it's home video release. A remake of the Howard Hawk's classic, 12 people in a US station in Antartcica are put in the mits of a alien with the ability to absord and take the form of their victim. The paranoia sets in as the close encounter with the "Thing" kicks things into high gear in this gloomy and dramatic remake....

After Hours(1985)

Protaganist Paul Hackett is driven from a taxi cab from hell to the dangerous and strange part of Soho, New York because of a fling with a girl in a diner. Put into the Hitchcock device of the "man falesly accused", Paul is put into bizarre situations that put a hinderence to him leaving Soho in the after hours of the night....

Lawrence of Arabia(1962)

The semi-biopic of T.E Lawrence and his time in Arabia with a tribe that are bent to take over a part of the country from the Turkish army. Lawrence is set as an above average man with a certain charm that wins the favor of Prince Feisal. Leading an army to take over parts of turkish occupied Arabia. With the stilling images of the beauty that director David Lean has put in his desert shots, this is a staple and apex of cinematic story telling and cinemtography....
